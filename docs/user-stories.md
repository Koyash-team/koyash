## Change log

- **2026-07-19:** Week 7 (Sprint 5) delivery. Marked US-11 (deterministic irritant warning, [#15](https://github.com/Koyash-team/koyash/issues/15)) and US-27 (password reset, [#159](https://github.com/Koyash-team/koyash/issues/159)) `Done`, shipped in the final `MVP v3` release ([`v1.4.0`](https://github.com/Koyash-team/koyash/releases/tag/v1.4.0)). Also corrected the Work Status of the Sprint 4 account stories that were delivered in the `v1.3.0` trial but still showed `To Do` — US-12, US-13, US-21, US-22, US-24, US-25, US-26 → `Done`.
- **2026-07-17:** Reworked US-11 (irritant warning, [#15](https://github.com/Koyash-team/koyash/issues/15)) into a **deterministic** mini-version ([PBI-505](https://github.com/Koyash-team/koyash/issues/184)) and pulled it into active Sprint 5 work. The original idea (ours) was to generate the warning via the LLM prompt, but we are keeping the LLM **justification-only** (ADR-001). Most of the risk is already covered deterministically — declared allergens and the special-condition filter (US-20) hard-exclude irritating ingredients — so the warning only covers the remaining gap: a **sensitive-skin** user (skin type `sensitive` or the `sensitivity` concern) who did not declare those and still gets a suitable product carrying a common irritant (fragrance, drying alcohol, a retinoid, or a strong acid). It is a rule-based patch-test heads-up on the product card (results + saved bag), not an exclusion and not an LLM output.
